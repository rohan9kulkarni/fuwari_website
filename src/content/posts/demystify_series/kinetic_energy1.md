$E_{\text{kinetic}}=\frac{1}{2}mv^2$ yeah? But, why?

Well as a starters, this is just the first term in a much longer series, but the remaining terms go as $\mathcal{O}(v/c)$. So unless we have velocities that are relativistic, i.e. $v$ approaching the speed of light $c$, we can forget about those terms.
